We are known for :

Papa John's story began in 1984 when John Schnatter, the founder, sold his beloved 1971 Camaro to fund his dream of making better pizza. He used the money to purchase used restaurant equipment, setting up shop in a broom closet in his father's tavern in Jeffersonville, Indiana. From that modest space, he started crafting pizzas with a focus on high-quality ingredients and fresh dough. His dedication to quality quickly gained popularity, and within a year, Papa John's opened its first official restaurant. As the brand expanded, it became known for its signature phrase, "Better Ingredients, Better Pizza," reflecting its commitment to superior taste and quality. Over the years, Papa John's has grown into one of the largest pizza chains globally, with thousands of stores in numerous countries, but it has never lost its focus on delivering fresh, high-quality pizzas to its customers.

Did papa johns win any awardas ?

Yes, Papa John's has received several awards over the years, particularly for its focus on quality and customer satisfaction. Some notable recognitions include:

  • Pizza Today's Chain of the Year – Papa John's has been named "Chain of the Year" multiple times by Pizza Today magazine, a prestigious award in the pizza industry.
  • American Customer Satisfaction Index (ACSI) – For several consecutive years, Papa John's has ranked highly in the ACSI for customer satisfaction, particularly in categories like food quality and delivery service.
  • Brand of the Year – Papa John's has been recognized as "Brand of the Year" in the Pizza Restaurant category by the Harris Poll EquiTrend study, which measures consumer opinions on quality, familiarity, and purchase consideration.
